Indian Tacos

Basically a taco made with frybread

first you make the frybread, which will probably take the most time. The next step is to cook the hamburger. Then gather all the extra toppings you would like to add

The last step would be to build it like you would a normal taco. Only the main difference is your eating it with frybread.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • yeast
  • hamburger
  • sour cream
  • lettuce
  • diced tomatos
  • olives

Steps

  1. combine flour yeast
  2. cook hamburger
  3. add everything on top of frybread when ready
