Salsa

Mushed up vegetables and stuff

Salsa involes a mish mash of different kind of vegetables and spices. I'm not really sure how to make it, but i'll try my best to explain.

Ingredients

  • tamatos
  • your choice of peppers
  • onions
  • garlic
  • lime

Steps

  1. dice the tomatos
  2. dice the onions
  3. dice the garlic
  4. mix all the diced thing together
  5. smash everything in a bowl
  6. squeeze the lime juice in the mix
