Spaghetti

Cooked pasta with sauce

Pretty easy to make not much cooking exerience required. The hardest part of doing this would be making sure you don't overcook the pasta.

Ingredidents

  • spaghettti pasta
  • pre-made marinara sauce
  • hamburger
  • seasonig of your choice
  • parmesan cheese/powder

Steps

  1. boil a pot of water
  2. cook spaghetti
  3. cook hamburger
  4. keep a very close eye on both
  5. strain spaghetti when done
  6. strain hamburger if very greasy
  7. pour marinara on hamburger in pot on medium temp
  8. mix all together when marinara is hot
  9. add permesan to each serving and enjoy
